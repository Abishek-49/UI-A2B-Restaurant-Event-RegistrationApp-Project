Paratha :  Nutrient Boost, Whole wheat flour parathas provide critical nutrients such as B vitamins (B1, B2, B3, B6), which are important for energy metabolism and nervous system function.

Making & Sides
  1. Typically Parathas are simple flatbreads that are made with whole wheat (or atta) flour
  2. A soft dough is prepared by hand, formed and flattened,
  3. Added with some oil or ghee and water.
  4. Then folded several times to create lots of flaky and Crispy layers.
  5. Then,It is served with curries (dal or vegetables)
