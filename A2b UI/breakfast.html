IDLY : What happens if we eat idli daily? Is Idli healthy, Is Idli Good for Weight Loss? Idli is a perfect light and nutritious meal which makes a complete way for protein in our body. It additionally is without gluten, so if you need to go gluten free, Idli would be the best option for you. It is also a low calorie recipe, thus making it a good option for people on weight loss.Also,the absence of saturated fats in idlis makes them heart-friendly, contributing to a well-balanced diet. One Idli has only 33 Calories

Making & Sides
  1. Rice and black gram were soaked separately,
  2. After draining the water, rice and black gram were grind separately with occasional addition of water during grinding process,
  3. The rice and black gram batters were then mixed together with addition of a little salt,Then followed by all these process .....
  4. Finally, It is poured into a food steamer, kitchen equipment designed expressly to cook food with steam.
  5. Then,it is served with sambar and cocnut chutney

DOSA : Easy on your Digestive System. Dosas are not just a treat for your taste buds, but also light on your digestive system. The Best Source of Carbohydrates. It is also a great source of healthy carbohydrates. Also Good Source of Protein. Low on Fat, and increases the bioavailability of nutrients which helps the body toassimilate more nutrition.

Making & Sides
  1. It is made with fermented rice and lentil batter
  2. Soaking 2 cups of rice, ½ cup urad dal and ½ tsp fenugreek seeds for 4-5 hours
  3. The rice and black gram batters were then mixed together with addition of a little salt,Then followed by all these process .....
  4. Finally, It is poured into a heating pan added with and some teaspoon of oil ,(pan) kitchen equipment designed expressly to make dosa,
  5. Then,it is served with sambar and cocnut chutney
